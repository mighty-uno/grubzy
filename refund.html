Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 06, 2026

1. Supporter Donations

Financial contributions made via the Razorpay payment gateway on this portal represent voluntary support donations made directly to help develop and maintain the Grubzy cravings simulator project.

2. Non-Refundable Contributions

Because support payments are completed as voluntary software donations, all contributions are final and non-refundable.

3. Virtual Purchases

Grubzy does not sell real-world items, food dishes, or digital features for real money. Any virtual items checked out inside the simulator require zero spending. Since zero payment is charged during application orders, no refunds exist or apply for simulated deliveries.

4. Exception Inquiries

If you believe a payment was executed in error due to gateway discrepancies, please contact your processing banking institution or email us at grubzy.dev@zohomail.in with transaction identifiers.
