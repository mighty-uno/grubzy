Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 06, 2026

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Because Grubzy is designed as a local offline cravings simulator, we do not require user account logins, phone numbers, or credit card information for checkouts. No personal user data is sent to, or cached on, remote cloud servers.

2. Local Database Storage

The application uses a local SQLite database sandboxed on your device to save order checkouts, stats (dodged calories, money saved, active levels, and CraveCoins), and dynamic rider coordinates. This data remains completely under your control and is cleared instantly if you delete the application.

3. Payment Contributions

Financial donations made through Razorpay links are processed securely by Razorpay. Grubzy does not collect, capture, or store your payment details (e.g. credit/debit card numbers, CVV, or banking credentials).

4. Security Guidelines

Since data storage is confined to your local hardware, securing your device directly protects your local metrics and virtual order history. We recommend keeping your mobile OS up to date.

5. Contact Information

For any clarifications regarding Grubzy's local database operations, please reach out to us at grubzy.dev@zohomail.in.
