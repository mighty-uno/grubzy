Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 06, 2026

1. Simulated Order Cancellations

All checkouts placed inside the Grubzy food delivery simulator are instant local operations. Because they are visual simulations carrying zero real-world cost and zero physical meal preparation, order checkouts cannot be canceled, modified, or recalled once placed.

2. Support Contributions Cancellation

Financial contributions made via Razorpay support links represent voluntary one-time support donations. Once a transaction is completed, it cannot be canceled, scheduled for cancellation, or reversed.

3. Contact Support

If you have questions regarding the checkout pipeline or transaction logs, feel free to reach out to us at grubzy.dev@zohomail.in.
